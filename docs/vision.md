## Overview

Capytal is an investment wallet management platform focused on helping investors
understand, organize and rebalance their assets through a
simple and intuitive experience.

The project aims to provide clear investment wallet visualization, intelligent
allocation insights and long-term investment planning tools for users
investing primarily in stocks and real estate assets.

---

## Problem

Many retail investors track their investments manually through spreadsheets
or fragmented platforms that do not provide clear assets allocation
visibility or rebalancing guidance.

Managing long-term investment strategies becomes difficult as assets grow,
especially when users want to maintain target allocations across different
asset classes and tickers.

---

## Vision

Capytal seeks to simplify assets management by offering a centralized
investment wallet where users can:

- Track holdings and allocations
- Visualize assets distribution
- Understand how much to invest in each asset
- Plan long-term rebalancing strategies
- Manage investment growth more clearly and confidently

The long-term vision is to create a modern and accessible platform that
helps investors make more informed decisions without unnecessary
complexity.

---

## Target Users

Capytal is initially focused on:

- Beginner and intermediate investors
- Long-term investors
- Users investing in stocks and REITs
- Users seeking assets organization and allocation clarity

---

## Phase 0 — Engineering Foundation

The initial phase of the project focuses on building the technical
foundation of the platform and showcasing software engineering and QA
practices.

This phase includes:

- Authentication system
- Testing architecture
- CI/CD workflows
- Project structure and infrastructure
- Initial UI foundation

---

## Phase 1 — MVP

The first product MVP focuses on delivering the core assets management
experience.

Initial features include:

- Wallet creation
- Asset and holdings management
- Assets allocation visualization
- Buy target calculations
- Stock and REIT support

---

## Future Goals
